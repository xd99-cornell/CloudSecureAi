Empowering Small & Mid-Size Businesses With Smart Resource Management

CouldSecureAI is a non-profit platform designed to help small and medium businesses efficiently manage their cloud resources, reduce costs, and enhance security. Get enterprise-level tools at no cost to grow your business sustainably.

Simplicity, visibility, cost control, and security — without needing experts.

Why Choose CouldSecureAI?

Free, comprehensive resource management tools designed specifically for small and medium businesses

Smart Cloud Protection

Automatically detect and protect your data across AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud — without needing a full-time security team. Simple, AI-driven defense for small businesses.

Unified Cloud Dashboard

View all your cloud accounts, apps, and security status in one clean, easy-to-read dashboard. No more switching between multiple consoles or confusing reports.

One-Click Security Scan

Run quick cloud security scans anytime. Instantly find weak passwords, risky settings, or open ports — and get guided steps to fix them.

Compliance Made Simple

Stay aligned with industry standards like GDPR, SOC2, and HIPAA — even if you don't have a compliance officer. Automated checks and reports make audits stress-free.

AI Insights & Recommendations

Let AI help you make smart decisions. Get personalized tips on improving your cloud security posture — tailored to your business size and cloud setup.

Free & Non-Profit Support for SMBs

We're here to help, not sell. Cloud Secure AI is a community-driven, non-profit project offering small businesses enterprise-level cloud protection — at zero cost.
